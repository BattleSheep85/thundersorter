Thundersorter

Before you start, please review how Thundersorter handles your data.

What data is sent

To classify your emails, Thundersorter sends the following to the AI provider you choose:

S
Email subject line
The subject of each email being classified.
F
Sender address
The "From" field (name and email address).
B
Email body (up to 4,000 characters)
The text content of the email. Only the first 4,000 characters are sent. Attachments are never sent.
T
Your tag list
The category names you've configured (e.g., "finance", "newsletters"). No personal data.

What Thundersorter does NOT do

  • Does not store your emails outside of Thunderbird
  • Does not send attachments, images, or file contents
  • Does not send any account credentials or passwords
  • Does not track you -- no analytics, no cookies, no device IDs
  • Does not share data with anyone other than your chosen AI provider
  • Does not work in the background without your knowledge -- you control when classification runs
  • Automatically skips security-sensitive emails (password resets, verification codes, 2FA) -- they are never sent to the AI

Your data is sent directly to the AI provider you select (e.g., Google Gemini, OpenAI, Anthropic, or others). Each provider has their own privacy policy. Thundersorter does not operate any servers and does not see your data. All connections use HTTPS encryption (except local providers like Ollama which stay on your machine).

If you decline, Thundersorter will not send any data. You can enable it later in the add-on preferences.
